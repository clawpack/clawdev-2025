Clawpack Developers' Workshop and Hackathon

October 6-8, 2025

www.clawpack.org/clawdev-2025


Description of the workshop

The workshop takes place October 6-8, 2025 at the University of Washington and will focus on collaborations between core Clawpack developers, their students, and users with new development needs.

It immediately follows the 5th Biennial Meeting of the Pacific Northwest Section of SIAM (Jointly with the 35th Pacific Northwest Numerical Analysis Seminar).

The Github repository clawpack/clawdev-2025 contains these webpages and can also be used for sharing resources.

The wiki in this repository can be used for suggesting projects (preferably with links to relevant Github Issues).

On Monday October 6 we will meet in Lewis Hall 208, the Wan Conference room. Plans for the following days are not fixed.

If you are interested in participating please contact Randy LeVeque, (rjl at uw.edu)
