Clawpack Developers' Workshop and Hackathon

October 6-7, 2025

www.clawpack.org/clawdev-2025


Description of the workshop

The workshop takes place October 6-7, 2025 at the University of Washington and will focus on collaborations between core Clawpack developers, their students, and users with new development needs.

It immediately follows the 5th Biennial Meeting of the Pacific Northwest Section of SIAM (Jointly with the 35th Pacific Northwest Numerical Analysis Seminar).

The Github repository clawpack/clawdev-2025 contains these webpages and can also be used for sharing resources.

The wiki in this repository can be used for suggesting projects (preferably with links to relevant Github Issues). For more info and to suggest specific topics to explore with them, see this wiki pag.

If you are interested in participating please contact Randy LeVeque, (rjl at uw.edu)

Tentative Schedule

  • Monday October 6: We will meet at 9:00am in Lewis Hall 208, the Wan Conference room.

  • Tuesday October 7: We will meet at 9:00am in the meeting room of the eScience WRF Data Science Studio on the 6th floor of the Physics & Astronomy Tower on the southwest corner of the University of Washington campus. It's a nice space and also will give us a chance to talk with the eScience Data Scientists and/or the UW Scientific Software Engineering Center (SSEC), folks who have expertise on many topics related to Clawpack and GeoClaw development.
